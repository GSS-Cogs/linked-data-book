Welcome

This is the website for the work-in-progress “Creating Linked Open Statistical Data”. This book will teach producers of data and statistics how to turn their data into 5⭐ linked open data.

Since 2016, the UK’s Office for National Statistics (ONS) have supported the Government Statistical Service (GSS) in proving the potential for statistical data to be transformed into 5⭐ linked open data. Our goal is to encourage data producers to create and share their data in a way that is more interoperable and reusable by using open standards.

This book serves two purposes:

  • To provide a practical guide for data and statistics producers to create 5⭐ linked open data by sharing our tools and methods.
  • To share our experiences and application of open standards with the wider linked open data community, so others may continue to build upon our work.
